Custom Advancement Tree

Skript-advancements is a Skript addon that allows players to manipulate advancements in Skript. With a variety of advancement features, skript-advancements offers more advancement elements than any other advancement addon. Not only does skript-advancements allow you to handle vanilla advancements in Minecraft, it also allows you to manipulate advancements from datapacks, other plugins, and even allows you to create custom advancements. Skript-advancements uses UltimateAdvancementsAPI to create and load advancements with packets, providing the fastest experience possible.
